Our Features

Of course there are a lot of similar products on the market. Hence we want to present our unique features.

Decentralized Data Storage

Through our decentralized communication and synchronization, 100% of your data is private.

Being open-source

Being open-source means that you as a customer are able to modify and improve existing code and hardware

Simplified starting process

In contrast to our competitors, you can track your time independently of your end-device. Just shake the cube and you’re in.

Intuitive interface

Providing easy to understand and detailed statistics of your time data is included too.

...and so much more!
